About

Est. 2026

GEVAUDAN produces dark essentials in limited quantities. No logos. No seasonal collections. No trend cycles.

Each garment is cut with precision from heavyweight fabrics — Italian leather, Japanese selvedge denim, Scottish wool. Materials that age with intent.

We work with small family-owned ateliers across Europe. Every piece is made in batches of 50 or fewer. Once it's gone, it's gone.

No advertising. No influencers. You found us or you didn't.

Production

Limited runs of 50 pieces max. Made in Portugal, Italy, and Scotland. No mass manufacturing.

Materials

Italian vegetable-tanned leather. Japanese raw denim. Scottish-spun wool. Hardware from Germany.

Shipping

Worldwide delivery. Free on orders over €300. Discreet packaging with no branding on the outside.
